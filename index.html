Simple Present Tense Rules

Formation:

  • For I, you, we, they: Use base form (play, work, study)
  • For he, she, it: Add -s/-es (plays, works, studies)

Spelling Rules:

  • Add -es to verbs ending in -s, -sh, -ch, -x, -o, -z
    • watch → watches
    • fix → fixes
    • go → goes
  • For verbs ending in consonant + -y:
    • study → studies
    • carry → carries

Uses:

  • Daily routines: "She works at 9 AM."
  • Habits: "They play soccer on weekends."
  • General truths: "The sun rises in the east."
  • Permanent situations: "He lives in London."
